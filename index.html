About Us

Where Every Meal
Feels Like Home

Hotel Sai Swad is Shirpur's beloved casual dining destination, offering authentic Indian cuisine in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Located opposite Polytechnic College at Karvand Naka, we serve dishes that remind you of home — from rich North Indian gravies and aromatic veg specials to sizzling Indo-Chinese favourites. 100% pure vegetarian. Our polite staff and comfortable seating make every visit a memorable one.
